## Subscriber Guide

**What is a Subscriber?**
Subscribers are customers who can browse products and place orders from the marketplace.

### Getting Started

#### 1. Create an Account
- Visit the login page
- Click "Create Account"
- Fill in your details:
  - Username (unique)
  - Email address
  - Password (at least 8 characters)
  - First and Last Name
- Click "Register"

#### 2. Set Up Your Profile
- Log in to your account
- Click "Edit Profile" in the left sidebar
- Add your contact information:
  - **Phone**: For delivery coordination
  - **Address**: Where you want orders delivered
  - **City/State/Zip**: For service area verification
- Click "Save Changes"

#### 3. Add Your Banking Information (Optional)
- Go to "Edit Profile"
- Scroll to "Banking Information" section
- Add your bank details if you plan to use payouts later
- Save your changes

### Browsing & Shopping

#### 1. Browse Products
- Click "Shop" in the main menu
- Browse products by category
- Click on a product to see details and price
- Check product availability

#### 2. Add to Cart
- Click "Add to Cart" on a product
- Adjust quantity if needed
- Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

#### 3. Checkout
- Click "View Cart"
- Review your items and total cost
- Select delivery method:
  - **Delivery**: Door-to-door service (optional)
  - **Pickup**: Pick up from market location
- Confirm your delivery address is correct
- Review estimated delivery cost
- Click "Place Order"

#### 4. Make Payment
- Your account balance will be checked
- If balance is sufficient, order is confirmed
- If balance is low, add funds first:
  - Click "Billing" → "Add Funds"
  - Choose payment method
  - Enter amount and complete payment

#### 5. Track Order
- Click "Orders" in left sidebar
- See order status: Pending → Confirmed → Processing → Delivered
- View estimated delivery time
- Contact support if you have questions

### Managing Your Account

#### Edit Profile
- Click "Edit Profile" in left sidebar
- Update any personal information:
  - Name
  - Email
  - Phone
  - Address
  - Banking info
- Click "Save Changes"

#### View Balance
- Click "Billing" in left sidebar
- See current account balance
- View payment history

#### Order History
- Click "Orders" in left sidebar
- See all past and current orders
- Filter by status or date

### Troubleshooting

**Q: Why can't I place an order?**
A: Your account balance may be insufficient. Go to Billing → Add Funds to top up your account.

**Q: How do I change my delivery address?**
A: Edit your profile with the new address. Each order can have a different delivery address.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: You can cancel pending orders. Once confirmed, contact support for cancellation requests.

## Market Agent Guide

**What is a Market Agent?**
Market agents operate their own marketplace stalls. They upload products, manage inventory, and fulfill orders.

### Getting Started

#### 1. Create a Business Account
- Register as "Market Agent"
- Set up your business profile:
  - **Business Name**: Your market/shop name
  - **Description**: What you sell and what makes you special
  - **Location**: Street address where you're located
  - **City/State/Zip**: Service area

#### 2. Set Up Banking Information
- Go to "Edit Profile"
- Add your banking details for receiving payments:
  - **Bank Name**
  - **Account Holder Name**
  - **Account Number**
  - **Branch Code**
  - **Account Type** (Savings, Cheque, Business)
- This is important for receiving payouts

### Managing Your Store

#### 1. Upload Products
- Click "My Products" in left sidebar
- Click "Upload Products"
- Fill in product details:
  - Product name
  - Description
  - Price
  - Category
  - Available quantity
  - Product image
- Click "Upload"

#### 2. Update Product Information
- Click "My Products"
- Click edit icon on any product
- Modify details (price, description, quantity)
- Save changes

#### 3. Manage Inventory
- Monitor stock levels in "My Products"
- Update quantity when stock changes
- Disable products that are out of stock
- Re-enable when back in stock

#### 4. Set Your Location
- Go to "Edit Profile"
- Ensure address is accurate and specific
- System uses this to calculate delivery distances
- Change address if you move locations

### Order Management

#### 1. View Orders
- Click "Orders" in left sidebar
- See all orders containing your products
- Filter by status (Pending, Confirmed, Delivered)

#### 2. Fulfill Orders
- Order comes in as "Pending"
- Verify items are in stock
- Click "Confirm" to prepare order
- Update status as you progress

#### 3. Delivery Integration
- If delivery is selected, a delivery person will be assigned
- Provide items to delivery person
- Confirm handoff in system

### Analytics & Reports

#### 1. Sales Dashboard
- Click "Dashboard" in left sidebar
- View sales today, this week, this month
- See best-selling products
- Track revenue

#### 2. Order Reports
- Click "Billing" → "Statements"
- View detailed order history
- See payout status
- Download statements for accounting

### Getting Paid

#### 1. How Payment Works
- Customers prepay via subscription
- When their order is delivered, funds are transferred to you
- Payments go to your bank account (weekly/monthly)

#### 2. Check Payout Status
- Click "Billing" → "Payouts"
- See pending and completed payouts
- Verify your banking information is correct

#### 3. Update Banking Information
- Click "Edit Profile"
- Go to "Banking Information"
- Update your account details if needed
- Save changes

### Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Keep products in stock
- Update prices fairly and clearly
- Provide accurate product descriptions
- Respond to customer inquiries quickly
- Maintain your location address

❌ **Don't:**
- List products you don't have
- Price discrimination
- Ignore order confirmations
- Provide incorrect delivery address
- Share your account credentials

## Delivery Person Guide

**What is a Delivery Person?**
Delivery persons pick up orders from markets and deliver them to customers.

### Getting Started

#### 1. Create a Delivery Account
- Register as "Delivery Person"
- Set up your profile:
  - Legal name
  - Phone number
  - Service area (where you deliver)
  - Address (home base location)

#### 2. Add Banking Information
- Go to "Edit Profile"
- Add your bank account details for receiving payments
- This must be completed before you can receive payouts

#### 3. Set Service Rates
- Click "Settings" → "Delivery Rates"
- Set your pricing:
  - **Base rate**: Fixed charge per delivery
  - **Per-km rate**: Price per kilometer
  - **Minimum charge**: Lowest amount you charge
- Save your rates

### Finding Deliveries

#### 1. View Available Orders
- Click "Available Orders" in left sidebar
- See orders needing delivery pickup
- View destination and pickup location
- Check estimated distance and payment

#### 2. Accept Order
- Click "Accept" on an order
- Confirm pickup location and time
- Get directions to market

#### 3. Pick Up Order
- Go to market location at scheduled time
- Confirm items with market agent
- Get customer delivery address
- Confirm payment estimate

### Making Deliveries

#### 1. Navigate to Customer
- Use your GPS/maps app
- View delivery address and customer details
- Estimated distance and time shown

#### 2. Deliver Order
- Arrive at customer location
- Get confirmation/signature if required
- Leave order at specified location
- Take a photo of delivery (if required)

#### 3. Complete Delivery
- Click "Mark as Delivered" in app
- Confirm delivery time
- Add any notes or issues
- Get customer acknowledgment if possible

### Managing Your Account

#### 1. View Earnings
- Click "Earnings" in left sidebar
- See daily, weekly, monthly totals
- View payment breakdown by delivery

#### 2. Track Hours
- System tracks delivery times automatically
- View hours worked per week
- Used for performance metrics

#### 3. Update Information
- Go to "Edit Profile"
- Update service area if you expand
- Update banking info if account changes

### Performance & Ratings

#### 1. Customer Ratings
- Customers rate delivery service
- Aim for 4.5+ star rating
- Poor ratings may restrict your access

#### 2. On-Time Performance
- System tracks delivery timeliness
- Late deliveries may impact ratings
- Plan routes to avoid delays

#### 3. Communication
- Respond to customer inquiries quickly
- Update status if running late
- Professional communication is important

## Administrator Guide

**What is an Administrator?**
Administrators manage the entire platform, users, billing, and system settings.

### Admin Dashboard Access

#### 1. Access Admin Panel
- Go to `http://localhost:8000/admin`
- Log in with your superuser credentials
- You now have full system access

### User Management

#### 1. Manage Users
- Click "Users" → "Custom Users"
- View all users and their types
- Edit user information
- Change user status (active/inactive)
- Reset user passwords

#### 2. Create Test Users
- Click "Add User"
- Enter username and password
- Select user type
- Add optional information
- Save user

#### 3. View User Details
- Click on any user
- View profile information
- See account creation date
- Check verification status

### Product Management

#### 1. View All Products
- Click "Market" → "Products"
- See all products across all markets
- Check inventory levels
- View pricing

#### 2. Manage Categories
- Click "Market" → "Product Categories"
- Create new categories
- Edit/delete categories
- Organize product catalog

#### 3. Monitor Inventory
- View product stock levels
- Flag low-stock items
- Monitor product uploads

### Order Management

#### 1. View All Orders
- Click "Orders" → "Orders"
- See all orders in system
- Filter by status, date, market
- View order details

#### 2. Update Order Status
- Click on order
- Change status as needed
- Add notes or comments
- System notifies relevant parties

#### 3. Cancel Orders
- Only cancel if necessary
- Provide reason
- Notify customer/market
- Handle refunds if needed

### Billing Administration

#### 1. Create Billing Plans
- Click "Billing" → "Billing Plans"
- Create subscription tiers
- Set pricing and features
- Determine duration

#### 2. Bill Subscribers
- Click "Billing" → "Admin Billing"
- Select subscriber
- Create monthly/custom bill
- Set due date
- Record payment

#### 3. View Transactions
- Click "Billing" → "Transactions"
- See all account movements
- Filter by user, type, date
- Export reports

#### 4. Manage Payouts
- Click "Billing" → "Payouts"
- Approve pending payouts
- Process payments to vendors
- Track payout history

### System Settings

#### 1. Configure Currency
- Go to Settings
- Set default currency
- Update exchange rates if needed

#### 2. Set Delivery Settings
- Configure delivery zones
- Set default delivery rates
- Manage service areas

#### 3. Email Configuration
- Set up email notifications
- Configure email templates
- Test email delivery

### Reports & Analytics

#### 1. Sales Reports
- View total sales
- Revenue by market agent
- Revenue by product category
- Seasonal trends

#### 2. User Metrics
- New user registrations
- Active users
- User retention
- Churn analysis

#### 3. Financial Reports
- Revenue summary
- Payout summary
- Transaction history
- Tax information

### Troubleshooting & Support

#### 1. Resolve User Issues
- Reset forgotten passwords
- Unlock locked accounts
- Verify user information
- Handle account disputes

#### 2. Manage Disputes
- View reported issues
- Investigate complaints
- Make decisions
- Document resolutions

#### 3. Maintenance
- Monitor system performance
- Check error logs
- Clear expired data
- Backup database

### Security Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Use strong passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly backup data
- Monitor suspicious activity
- Keep system updated

❌ **Don't:**
- Share admin credentials
- Use default passwords
- Delete production data
- Make untested changes
- Grant admin access lightly
